A reading circle · College Lane, Oxenford

Reading by candlelight, thinking in centuries.

“Vita sine litteris mors est.” — Life without letters is death. Seneca · Epistulae Morales, LXXXII

Since 1892 the Society has kept the same three rules: arrive before dusk, annotate in pencil, and never shelve a book you have not argued with. The reading room seats twenty-four; the silence seats more.
