Inside the studio

Forged the hard way

Our toolchain is a tile editor from 1994, a four-channel tracker, and a wall of graph paper. Nothing here is generated and nothing is smoothed; if a pixel ships, somebody placed it on purpose — and probably defended it in a meeting.

Glorp, the office slime, handles morale and playtests the water levels.
