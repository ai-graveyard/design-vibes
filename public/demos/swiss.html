Programme — five rooms, one grid

P.02 / 04
Room A — Ground floor

Type as Image

Letterforms enlarged until they stop reading and start standing. Sixty posters, none illustrated, all typographic.

Room B — Ground floor

The Grid Is a Decision

Working layouts, column studies and paste-ups showing structure decided before content arrives.

Room C — Mezzanine

Objective Photography

The camera as measuring instrument. No retouching, no sentiment, full caption discipline.

Room D — Mezzanine

Red as Signal

One accent colour, used once per page. A study of restraint across four decades of timetables and wayfinding.

Room E — Print workshop

Setting by Hand, Daily 14:00

A working composing room. Visitors set one line of metal type and pull their own proof on the flatbed press.
