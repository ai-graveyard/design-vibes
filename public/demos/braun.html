01 Product M 200 — Control Amplifier

Every control does exactly one thing.

The M 200 amplifies a signal. It does not interpret it, flatter it, or decorate it. Two rotary controls, three switches, one indicator — nothing that is not necessary.

03 Design Working principles

Less, but better.

3.1

Honest

The M 200 does not promise more than it delivers. Its specifications are measured, not marketed.

3.2

Useful

Nothing on the panel is decoration. When a function is removed, its control is removed with it.

3.3

Long-lived

Designed to be repaired, not replaced. Every fastener is reachable with one standard tool.
