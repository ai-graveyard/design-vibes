02

A Hundred Years, Five Rooms

Hundert Jahre Form — Centenary retrospective
  • 1919

    The founding manifesto

    Fourteen students, one borrowed building, and a single page declaring that the artist and the craftsman are the same person on different days.

  • 1923

    First public exhibition

    The workshops open their doors. Critics arrive to laugh and leave carrying chairs.

  • 1926

    The glass wing

    A new building of steel and daylight — the curriculum poured into concrete, with the stairwell as lecture hall.

  • 1968

    The archive returns

    Two thousand drawings, banned, scattered and posted home in tubes, are reunited under one catalogue number.

  • 2026

    The workshop century

    All five rooms rehung. Original benches, working presses, and the Grundkurs taught daily at noon — open to anyone with clean hands.
